Initial recommendation:

- Python/FastAPI backend for parsing, rules, imports, analytics, and later optimization.
- PostgreSQL database.
- TypeScript/Vite frontend, initially framework-light.
- Start with import + analysis + validation + leave management before full automatic rota generation.

Confirmed MVP direction:

- Admin-only first version.
- Both import/analysis and new rota creation.
- Excel export is required.
- Historical Jan 2025-May 2026 import as seed data.
- At least 24 hours between two 24-hour duties.
- Duty limits configurable from admin panel.
