## Important Principles

- Do not merge people only because they share a first name.
- Single-token aliases such as `Sam`, `Daniel`, `Rohan`, `Naveen`, `Preethi`, `Jeenu`, `Priya`, `Anisha`, `Angeline`, and `Jonathan` require duty/call context unless a full variant is present.
- Historical inactive people may exist as canonical historical names for analysis, even if they are not active department members now.
- Placeholder names such as `XXXXX`, `XXXXXX`, and `XXXXXXX` are not real people and remain skipped.
- January 2025 `C/S AM` is Caesar A, and `Caesar Call PM` is Caesar B.
- CART `Jeenu` is always `Jeenu Ann Jose`.
